PWA Builder Checklist

1. Remove the inline base64 manifest from your HTML.
2. Save manifest.json beside your HTML.
3. Save service-worker.js beside your HTML.
4. Create an icons folder containing:
   - icon-192.png
   - icon-512.png
   - maskable-512.png
5. Add the integration snippet to your HTML.
6. Test locally and then upload to PWA Builder.
